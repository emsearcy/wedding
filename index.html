The Plans

Eric and Alicia will be married on the 10th of May, 2014 in Corvallis, Oregon.

They are looking forward to getting to stay in Corvallis, where Eric currently works from home for the Linux Foundation, and Alicia has recently finished at Oregon State University with a degree in biology.

The Location

They will be married at their church in Corvallis, Westminster Presbyterian Church, where Eric also serves as a deacon. For directions to Westminster and other information to help you plan your travels, please visit the directions page.

Gift registries are available to help pick out something for the new couple.

The Couple

Eric and Alicia got to know each other while attending Westminster Presbyterian Church in Corvallis (Eric frequently gave Alicia rides to church). He asked her out to have gelato at Francesco's in February of last year, and before long she said yes to his proposal of marriage in September.

They enjoy social ballroom dancing, cooking, playing board games, and, sometimes, even doing yard work together.

FAQ

Q. Can I bring my children?
A. Yes! Whether you received an invitation or are a part of one of our church "families," then your immediate family or household is invited as well.
Q. Can I bring my significant other?
A. Yes, you are welcome to bring a "plus one" with you. Just RSVP accordingly, please!
Q. Are the venues wheelchair accessible?
A. Both venues should be accessible for you. In particular, at the reception, you are welcome to be dropped off in your vehicle at the bottom of the ramp at the back of the church building (which is the same entrance to the building everyone will use).
Q. Should I be concerned if I have an allergy to anything, like peanuts, gluten, etc.?
A. We'll try to have a variety of snack food options which will be well-labeled.
