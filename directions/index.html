Directions

Directions to Westminster Presbyterian from Interstate 5

The Corvallis exit is number 228 for Corvallis / Hwy 34. Coming from the north, take a right onto Hwy 34 towards Corvallis. Coming from the south, take a left onto Hwy 34 (towards Corvallis).

In 10 miles, turn right on 3rd St (Hwy 99W northbound). In 4 miles, take a left on Lewisburg Ave (by the Texaco gas station). In a mile, take a left onto NW Highland Dr.

The church will be in a half mile on your left: 5005 NW Highland Dr.

Other ways to get there

You can come in from the coast and get on 3rd St heading North if you take the Downtown Corvallis exit from Hwy 34 (that stretch also happens to be Hwy 20).

You can come down from Portland on Hwy 99W, too, for a more scenic route than I-5.

Local attractions

http://visitcorvallis.com/ has a lot of information on hotels and restaurants. If you are interested in local breweries or distilleries, this "sip trip" pamphlet highlights some local breweries and distilleries: https://visitcorvallis.com/dining/mid-valley-sip-trip-2/
